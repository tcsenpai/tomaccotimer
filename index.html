How Tomacco works

Inverse Pomodoro. Instead of fixed work/break blocks, your break is earned from what you actually worked.

  1. Start working — timer counts up while you focus.
  2. Stop & take break — you earn worked ÷ ratio of break time (ratio default 5 = one fifth).
  3. Break counts down, beeps + notifies when done.
  4. Reset abandons the current session. Pop out floats an always-on-top window (Chromium only).
